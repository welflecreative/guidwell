Quick-Start Guide

Up and running in 10 minutes

Install the plugin, customize the starter wizard, and embed it on any page. No developer setup required — just your WordPress admin and a few clicks.

1. Installation

Guidwell is a standard WordPress plugin delivered as a .zip file. No hosting changes, no server access, no code.

Download & Upload

  1. After purchase, download the guidwell.zip file from your confirmation email or account dashboard.
  2. In your WordPress admin, go to Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin.
  3. Click Choose File, select the guidwell.zip you downloaded, then click Install Now.

Activate

  1. Once the upload finishes, click Activate Plugin.
  2. Done. A Guidwell menu item now appears in your WP Admin sidebar.
✅
Future updates are automatic. WordPress will detect new Guidwell releases and show an update prompt in your Plugins list — just like any other plugin.

2. First Launch

What You'll See

Click Guidwell in the sidebar. You'll land on the Wizard Builder — a split-panel editor with questions on the left and a live preview on the right.

Starter Template

A sample wizard is pre-loaded with placeholder questions and plans so you can see exactly how everything fits together. A blue banner at the top of the screen confirms it — this starter wizard hasn't been saved yet, so feel free to edit everything before committing anything to your database.

ℹ️
The starter template is a great starting point for agencies and consultants. You'll replace the sample text with your own services — typically takes 5–10 minutes.

3. Build Your Wizard

The Wizard Builder has two parts: Questions (what visitors answer) and Plans (what you recommend). Edit both, then save.

Edit Questions

  1. Click any question in the left panel to open it for editing.
  2. Update the question text and each answer label to reflect your services. Add or remove answers as needed.
  3. Set a weight (0–10) for each answer under each plan. Higher weight means that answer is a stronger signal toward recommending that plan.
✅
Keep questions outcome-focused. Ask about budget, team size, goals — things that genuinely separate your service tiers. Avoid questions where every answer maps to the same plan.

Edit Plans

  1. Click a plan name in the lower section of the left panel.
  2. Fill in the plan name, price or tagline, short description, and the CTA button label and URL (e.g., your booking link or sales page).
  3. Repeat for each plan tier.

Scoring Weights

When a visitor completes the wizard, Guidwell totals the weights for all their chosen answers across each plan. The plan with the highest total is recommended. You can review and fine-tune these weights directly in the builder — each answer row shows a weight field per plan.

⚠️
Save before navigating away. There is no auto-save. Click Save in the top-right of the builder tab when you're done — the button greys out to confirm your changes are stored.

4. Embed the Wizard

Once your wizard is saved, place it on any page in seconds. Three methods — pick whichever fits your setup.

Shortcode (fastest)

Edit any page or post and paste this into the content area:

[guidwell]

That's it. Guidwell automatically uses your published wizard. Publish or update the page and the wizard is live.

Gutenberg Block

In the block editor, click the + block inserter and search for Guidwell. Insert the block anywhere on the page. The block includes a sidebar control to choose which wizard to display if you have more than one.

Elementor Widget

In Elementor, search for Guidwell in the widget panel and drag it onto your canvas. The widget settings let you select your wizard and adjust display options without leaving the editor.

ℹ️
The wizard loads asynchronously, so it won't slow down your page. It's safe to embed inside page builder sections, sidebars, or popups.

5. Go Live

Before you share the page, run through this quick checklist on the frontend as a regular visitor would.

Checklist

  • The wizard card appears and shows your first question
  • Selecting an answer highlights it and enables the Next button
  • Progressing through all questions opens the results modal
  • The modal shows the correct recommended plan
  • The CTA button links to the right URL
  • The modal closes with the × button, Escape key, or clicking the overlay
✅
Spot-check your scoring. Choose a specific path through your wizard (same answers each time) and verify the right plan wins. If results seem off, revisit the weight values in the builder.

You're live.

Share the page link, add it to your nav, or embed the wizard in a landing page. Every visitor who completes the quiz gets a personalized plan recommendation — and you get a lead who already knows which tier fits them best.
